Licensing history for files in assets/Textures/skies/moon

The files "full.png", "waning-crescent.png", "waning-gibbous.png",
"waxing-crescent.png", and "waxing-gibbous.png" are derived from frames
32, 48, 38, 14, and 25 of an animation downloaded in 2013 from
https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Lunar_libration_with_phase_Oct_2007.gif
where it was described as the work of Tom Ruen, who had released it into the
public domain.

The file "clementine.jpg" is derived from a digital image downloaded in
2014 from http://www.nrl.navy.mil/clm/  As a work prepared by the United States
Naval Research Laboratory, it is presumed to be in the public domain.
